This website uses only the cookies and technical logs necessary for its proper operation.

## 1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit certain web pages. They help websites remember certain information and function correctly.

---

## 2. Cookies we use

This site is hosted on **GitHub Pages**, which may generate technical logs and use mechanisms necessary to:

- Allow access to and display of content.  
- Maintain the security and stability of the service.

We do **not** use our own cookies for advertising or advanced analytics.

---

## 3. Third-party cookies

When you click on links that lead to external sites (such as Amazon), those websites may use their own cookies for functional, statistical, or advertising purposes.

Little Promise Press has no control over third-party cookies and recommends that you check their respective cookies and privacy policies.

---

## 4. Managing cookies

You can configure your browser to block or delete cookies.  
The specific steps depend on the browser you use (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.), usually in the **Privacy** or **Cookies** settings section.

Please note that disabling certain essential cookies may affect the correct functioning of some websites.
